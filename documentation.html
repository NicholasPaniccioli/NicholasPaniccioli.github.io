This is to show my process and things I'm working on

-Feburary 25th, 2021

As I'm writing this at approximately 1:13 AM one thing comes to mind, accomplishment. For the past two weeks or so I've been working on my portfolio website from scratch. While it would be as simple and easy to just find a template, post my resume on it and call it a day, I didn't want that. HTML and CSS are not my strong suits and I wanted to fix that. Challenging myself is the goal for this portfolio and so far I think I've done a great job.

As of this moment, I've successfully created cards that link to past projects and worked on page responsiveness that changes the size and layout of necessary elements. While that may not sound impressing, taming the beast that is CSS, for me is an achievement.

What's the plan now? Now I'm going to keep working on the portfolio. Clean up the CSS and make the overall appearance look more clean. I'm also going to work on my past projects and try to expand my skills with JavaScript.
